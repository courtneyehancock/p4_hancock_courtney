My Happy Life

Right now I am living my best life in Gainesville, FL in a little apartment complex called Towne Parc. I have my own backyard and mom and dad will sit outside with me while I roll in the grass. I am standing guard at all times - any peep and I am on the case.

I love chasing tennis balls... but I'm not very good at bringing them back.

Here are some fun facts about me

I like to run with my ears pinned back because I think it makes me go faster.

I'm a diehard Gators fan.

I'll occasionally decide to cheer on the New Jersey Devils and New York Mets per request of my parents.

Snuggling is my super power.

I have a small farting problem...

I snore in my sleep. Also sometimes when I'm awake.

I like to sleep under the covers.

My favorite treat is anything mom and dad are eating.

I love walking to the cows. They smell funny.

Please love me. I'll love you back.

Although I look like I could be mean, I am super sweet and human-friendly. When I see other pups, all I want to do is play! Please don’t discriminate against me just because of what I look like. My bark is scarier than my bite – and I don’t even bite! (Unless you’re my toy… then I’ll nibble). I feel so loved and I just want every living thing to be as loved as I am. I’ll even give you a lot of kisses.
